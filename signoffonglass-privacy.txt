OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━

Sign-on-Glass is a mobile application developed by Neeeeel Corp that
allows sole traders and tradespeople to capture client signatures on
their mobile device and generate professional PDF sign-off sheets.

Neeeeel Corp does not collect, transmit, store, or have any access
to any data entered into or generated by Sign-on-Glass. This policy
explains exactly what data the app uses, where it stays, and what
permissions it requests and why.


1. WHO WE ARE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Neeeeel Corp is the developer and publisher of Sign-on-Glass.

  Website:  www.neeeeel.com
  Email:    support@neeeeel.com

For any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at
support@neeeeel.com.


2. THE CORE PRINCIPLE: YOUR DATA NEVER LEAVES YOUR DEVICE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Sign-on-Glass is built as a 100% local-first application. This means:

  - We operate no servers or databases.
  - No data you enter is ever sent to Neeeeel Corp or any third party.
  - We have no ability to view, access, or recover your data.
  - The app works entirely offline with no internet connection required.

All information you enter — including your business name, client names,
job descriptions, and signature images — is stored exclusively on your
own device using the device's built-in on-device storage.


3. WHAT DATA THE APP STORES (ON YOUR DEVICE ONLY)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Sign-on-Glass stores the following information locally on your device:

  BUSINESS PROFILE
  Your business name, your name, contact phone number, your business
  logo image, and your default sign-off terms. This information is
  entered by you during setup and is used to populate your PDF
  sign-off sheets.

  JOB RECORDS
  For each sign-off you create: the client's name, the job address or
  postcode, a description of the work completed, the total cost
  (if entered), and the date and time of signing.

  SIGNATURE DATA
  The client's signature, captured as an image from the on-screen
  signature canvas. This image is stored on your device and embedded
  into your PDF sign-off sheets.

  GPS LOCATION DATA (OPTIONAL)
  If you grant location permission and GPS capture is available at the
  time of signing, the approximate latitude and longitude of your
  device at the moment of signing is stored with the job record and
  printed on the PDF. This data is stored on your device only. It is
  never transmitted to Neeeeel Corp.

None of the above data is ever transmitted to Neeeeel Corp or any
external server. It remains on your device at all times unless you
choose to share a PDF via the device's native share function.


4. PERMISSIONS THE APP REQUESTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

4.1 LOCATION (WHEN IN USE) — OPTIONAL
The app may request access to your device's location at the moment a
client signs a sign-off sheet. This is used solely to embed a GPS
coordinate on the PDF document as evidence that the work was
completed at the client's premises.

  - Location data is used only at the moment of signing.
  - It is stored on your device as part of the job record.
  - It is never transmitted to Neeeeel Corp or any third party.
  - You may decline this permission. The app functions fully without it.
  - Location is never accessed in the background.

4.2 PHOTO LIBRARY / CAMERA — OPTIONAL
The app requests access to your device's photo library solely to allow
you to select and upload your business logo. The logo image is stored
on your device and used only to personalise your PDF sign-off sheets.

  - No photos are transmitted to Neeeeel Corp or any third party.
  - Access is only triggered when you explicitly tap "Upload Logo".

4.3 ICLOUD DRIVE (iOS ONLY) — OPTIONAL
On iOS, the app may request permission to save PDF files to a
dedicated folder in your personal iCloud Drive account. This backup
is provided as a convenience to protect you against loss of data if
your device is lost, stolen, or replaced.

  - PDFs are backed up to YOUR iCloud account, not to Neeeeel Corp.
  - Neeeeel Corp has no access to your iCloud Drive.
  - You control your iCloud data entirely through your Apple ID.
  - You may disable this feature at any time in your device settings.

4.4 GOOGLE DRIVE (ANDROID ONLY) — OPTIONAL
On Android, the app may request permission to save PDF files to your
personal Google Drive account. This operates in the same way as the
iCloud backup described above.

  - PDFs are backed up to YOUR Google Drive, not to Neeeeel Corp.
  - Neeeeel Corp has no access to your Google Drive.
  - You control your Google Drive data through your Google Account.


5. PDF DOCUMENTS AND SHARING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PDF sign-off sheets are generated entirely on your device. When you
choose to share a PDF (via WhatsApp, Email, iMessage, or any other
app), you are doing so through your device's native share function.
Neeeeel Corp is not involved in this process and does not receive a
copy of any shared document.

You are responsible for how you share and store PDF documents
generated by Sign-on-Glass, including compliance with any applicable
data protection laws regarding the client information they contain.


6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Sign-on-Glass is a business application intended for use by adults
operating as sole traders or small business owners. It is not directed
at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in jurisdictions where that
threshold applies). We do not knowingly collect any information from
children. As we collect no information from any user, no special
provision is required beyond this statement.


7. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Sign-on-Glass does not integrate with any third-party analytics,
advertising, tracking, or data collection services. There are no
advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or crash reporting services
embedded in the application.

The PDF generation library (jsPDF) and signature capture library
(signature_pad) operate entirely on-device and do not communicate
with external servers.


8. DATA SECURITY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Because all data is stored locally on your device, the security of
your data depends primarily on the security of your device itself.
We recommend:

  - Using a device PIN, password, or biometric lock.
  - Keeping your device's operating system up to date.
  - Enabling device encryption (on by default on modern iOS and
    Android devices).

Neeeeel Corp cannot access, recover, or reset your data under any
circumstances.


9. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

You are in full control of your data.

  - Individual job records can be deleted from within the app at any
    time by opening the job and tapping the delete button.
  - All app data can be deleted by uninstalling Sign-on-Glass from
    your device.
  - Neeeeel Corp holds no copies of your data and cannot assist with
    data recovery.


10. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER UK AND EU DATA PROTECTION LAW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Under UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and similar data protection laws, individuals
have rights including the right to access, correct, and erase their
personal data, and the right to data portability.

Because Neeeeel Corp does not receive, process, or store any of your
personal data, we cannot fulfil subject access requests or erasure
requests on your behalf — your data does not exist on our systems.

All data is under your sole control on your device. You can exercise
your rights in full by managing or deleting data directly in the app
or by uninstalling the application.

If you use Sign-on-Glass to process personal data belonging to your
clients (such as their name, address, and signature), you may be
acting as a Data Controller under UK/EU GDPR in your own right. It
is your responsibility to ensure you handle your clients' personal
data lawfully, including informing them how their signature and details
will be stored and used.
