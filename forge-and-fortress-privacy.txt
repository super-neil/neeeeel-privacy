2. OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We take your privacy seriously. Forge & Fortress is a free game with no
advertisements and no in-app purchases. We do not sell your data, we do not
share it with advertisers, and we collect only the absolute minimum information
needed to operate the global leaderboard feature.

In short:
  - We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account
    information.
  - We do NOT track your location.
  - We do NOT use advertising identifiers (IDFA or similar).
  - We do NOT share or sell any data to third parties for commercial purposes.
  - The only information we ever receive is a player-chosen display name and a
    game score, submitted voluntarily at the end of a game session.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND WHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.1  LEADERBOARD DATA (voluntary)

When a game session ends, you are given the option to submit your score to the
global leaderboard. If you choose to do so, we collect:

  a) Display name
     - A name of your own choosing, typed by you at the point of submission.
     - This does not need to be your real name. You may use any pseudonym.
     - This name is displayed publicly on the in-game leaderboard.

  b) High score
     - Your numerical score from that game session.
     - Displayed publicly alongside your chosen display name on the leaderboard.

Leaderboard submission is entirely optional. The game is fully playable without
submitting a score. If you close or skip the submission prompt, nothing is sent.

3.2  TECHNICAL / NETWORK DATA

When your device connects to our leaderboard server to submit or retrieve scores,
our hosting infrastructure may automatically log:

  - IP address (used transiently for the network request; not stored against your
    leaderboard entry)
  - Date and time of the request
  - Device operating system type (iOS) and app version

This is standard server log data retained for up to 30 days for security and
troubleshooting purposes only. It is not linked to your display name or score.

3.3  WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect, and have no access to:
  - Your real name, email address, or any personal identifier
  - Apple ID or Game Center identity
  - Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, serial number)
  - Location data (GPS or approximate)
  - Health or fitness data
  - Contacts, messages, photos, or camera/microphone access
  - Browsing or search history
  - Financial information
  - Any data from other apps on your device

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The data described in Section 3 is used solely for the following purpose:

  - To display and maintain the global in-game leaderboard, so players can see
    how their scores compare with others.

We do not use your data for:
  - Marketing or advertising
  - Profiling or behavioural analysis
  - Selling or licensing to third parties
  - Any purpose other than operating the leaderboard

5. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING (GDPR — EEA/UK USERS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom,
we rely on the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation
(GDPR) / UK GDPR:

  - Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)):
    For transient server log data, to maintain the security and performance of
    our leaderboard service.

  - Consent (Article 6(1)(a)):
    For leaderboard name and score submission, which is a voluntary act you
    initiate by tapping the submit button. You can withdraw this consent at any
    time by requesting deletion of your entry (see Section 8).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6.1  Where data is stored

Leaderboard data (display name and scores) is stored on servers hosted by
Neeeeel Corp or its cloud infrastructure provider. Data may be stored in the
United Kingdom, the European Union, or the United States depending on our
hosting configuration at the time.

If data is transferred outside your jurisdiction, we ensure appropriate
safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable data protection law (for
EEA/UK users: Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms).

6.2  Security measures

We implement industry-standard technical measures to protect data in transit
and at rest, including:
  - HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between the app and our servers
  - Access controls limiting who within Neeeeel Corp can access leaderboard data

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100%
secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute
security.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. DATA RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - Leaderboard entries (display name + score) are retained for as long as the
    Forge & Fortress leaderboard service is active, or until you request
    deletion (see Section 8).

  - Server log data is retained for a maximum of 30 days and then deleted.

  - If the game is discontinued, all leaderboard data will be deleted within
    90 days of the service being taken offline.

8. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your
data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact: privacy@neeeeel.com

8.1  Right to Access
     You may request a copy of any data we hold about your leaderboard entry.

8.2  Right to Deletion / Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
     You may request that your leaderboard entry (display name and score) be
     deleted. Because no account or identifier is created, please include the
     display name and approximate score you submitted so we can locate your
     entry.

8.3  Right to Rectification
     You may request that an inaccurate entry be corrected.

8.4  Right to Object / Restrict Processing
     You may object to our processing of your data. In most cases this means
     requesting deletion of your leaderboard entry.

8.5  Right to Data Portability
     You may request your data in a machine-readable format.

8.6  Right to Withdraw Consent
     As leaderboard submission is voluntary, you can simply choose not to
     submit. For previously submitted entries, contact us for deletion.

8.7  California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)
     Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to know
     what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out
     of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
     To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@neeeeel.com

8.8  Response Time
     We will respond to all valid requests within 30 days (or within the
     timeframe required by applicable law).

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Forge & Fortress is rated 9+ on the Apple App Store. We do not knowingly
collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the
applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction, e.g. 16 in some EU
member states).

The leaderboard submission feature requires the user to voluntarily type and
submit a display name. We recommend that parents and guardians supervise
children's use of optional online features, including leaderboard submission.

If you believe a child under 13 has submitted information to our leaderboard
without appropriate parental consent, please contact us immediately at
privacy@neeeeel.com and we will promptly delete the relevant entry.

We do not use the app to direct targeted advertising at children, and we do
not collect data from children for commercial purposes.

10. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Forge & Fortress does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs, analytics
platforms (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude), or social media SDKs.

The only external network communication the app makes is to Neeeeel Corp's own
leaderboard API endpoint.

Phaser 3 (the game engine), Vite (build tool), and Capacitor (native wrapper)
are open-source libraries used to build the app. They do not independently
collect or transmit user data.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Forge & Fortress is distributed via the Apple App Store. Apple may collect
certain data as part of the App Store experience (e.g. download statistics,
crash reports via TestFlight). This data is governed by Apple's own Privacy
